About

The York Region Kiva is a community organization dedicated to raising money for entrepreneurs in developing nations affected by the COVID-19 pandemic. Through Kiva, an international micro-lending non-profit, we aim to raise $2020 by the end of 2020. Once we get back the money we lend, we aim to reloan our donations to further multiply our impacts.
